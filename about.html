Meet Fran

Francine Miller grew up surrounded by her grandmother's garden in the Outer Sunset, where she learned that flowers are more than decoration — they're a language. A way to say "I love you," "I'm sorry," "congratulations," and "I'm thinking of you" when words fall short.

After studying horticulture at UC Davis, Fran returned to San Francisco and opened her first small stand on Valencia Street in 1995 with nothing but a folding table, a bucket of dahlias, and a lot of heart.

Thirty years later, that stand has grown into a beloved neighborhood shop — but the philosophy hasn't changed. Every arrangement is made by hand, with care, and with the hope that it brings someone joy.

🌿 Locally Sourced

We partner with small farms across Northern California to bring you the freshest seasonal blooms possible.

❤ Community First

Ten percent of every sale goes toward Bay Area homelessness relief. Shopping here means giving back.

🌳 Sustainably Grown

We prioritize pesticide-free and eco-conscious growers so our flowers are good for you and the planet.
